Presentation

The department of computer science of the IUT in France is training students to computer science and teamwork so as to prepare them to their integration in professional life. It therefore offers a teaching supervised by teachers, professionals who teach students a practical and concrete experience of the IT sector.

The part dedicated to computer science of the teaching is based on 3 axes, each of them requiring specific qualities and skills:

-Tools and methods for software engineering :

That deals with the preliminary work in relation with the customer to specify his needs and expectations and the conception of the outline computer processing. This stage requires senses of

communication, open-mindedness, and analysis and synthesis...

-Algorithmic and programming:

The efficient implementation of the processing and its encoding ; this stage needs

a logical mind, and a technical accuracy...

-architecture system and network:

The understanding of how computer works, its operating system and the networks (The Internet, intranet...). This training part requires the following qualities:

Curiosity and patience...

To those teaching objectives, other general academic disciplines are added for half of the teaching learning time (Mathematics, Economy, Communication...), that provide a multidisciplinary aspect to the training.

Besides, the assessment is based on theory but also on practical situations, via supervised projects and an internship in a company enabling the validation the knowledge and skills acquired during the training.
